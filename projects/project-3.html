Problem Statement

Communities often lack efficient systems for disaster reporting and transparent fund management during crisis situations. Existing solutions may not provide comprehensive tracking or foster trust among donors and beneficiaries.

There's a need for a platform that combines disaster reporting, donation management, and transparent fund allocation to enhance community resilience and accountability.

Our Solution

A web-based disaster management platform that enables users to report disasters, make donations, and transparently track the allocation of funds. The platform aims to enhance community resilience and accountability in disaster response.

Key Features

Comprehensive disaster management capabilities

Disaster Reporting

Users can quickly report disasters in their area, providing real-time data to aid response efforts.

Transparent Donation Tracking

Donors can see how their contributions are being used, fostering trust and encouraging further support.

Fund Allocation System

Efficiently manage and allocate funds based on the needs arising from reported disasters.

Public Transparency
