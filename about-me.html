From Ha-Makhuvha to Success

Growing up in Ha-Makhuvha, Limpopo, was a unique experience shaped by the beauty of rural life and the strength of community. I attended Tshivhase Secondary School, where I cultivated my passion for learning and ultimately matriculated.

This foundation paved the way for my journey to Rosebank College, where I earned my IIE diploma. Each step of this journey has been instrumental in shaping who I am today - a dedicated software developer committed to creating meaningful solutions.

Pursuing My Passion for Software Development

My love for software development and technology keeps me motivated on my journey to becoming a tech lead one day. The challenges and innovations in this field inspire my creativity and drive me to push the boundaries of what's possible in tech.

Currently working as a Java Developer at FNB on the FXPH App team, I'm gaining valuable experience in enterprise-level financial applications while contributing to meaningful projects that impact millions of users.
